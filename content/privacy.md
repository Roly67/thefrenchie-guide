## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 15, 2026

### Introduction

The Frenchie Guide ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at https://thefrenchieguide.com (the "Site").

### Information We Collect

#### Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect:
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked
- **Device data:** Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Cookies:** See our Cookie Policy below

#### Information You Provide
We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as:
- Email address (if you subscribe to our newsletter)
- Name and contact details (if you contact us)
- Comments or feedback you submit

### How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Improve and personalise your experience on the Site
- Send newsletters or updates (if you've subscribed)
- Respond to your enquiries
- Analyse site traffic and user behaviour
- Comply with legal obligations

### Cookies

We use cookies to:
- Remember your preferences
- Analyse site traffic (via Google Analytics or similar tools)
- Enable affiliate link tracking

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

### Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect your information:

**Google Analytics**  
We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our Site. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.  
[Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Amazon Associates**  
As an Amazon Associate, we participate in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. Amazon may use cookies to track affiliate referrals.  
[Amazon Privacy Notice](https://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=201909010)

**Other Affiliate Networks**  
We may participate in other affiliate programmes (ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, etc.), which use cookies to track referrals.

### Advertising

We may display advertisements from Google AdSense or other ad networks. These services may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing activity.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting:
- [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- [Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out](http://optout.networkadvertising.org/)

### Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

### Your Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/UK, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Restrict or object to data processing
- Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@thefrenchieguide.com.

### Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on the Site or sending an email (if applicable).
